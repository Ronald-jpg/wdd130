Counrty: Uganda.

Uganda's history is one of pre-colonial kingdoms, British colonization from 1894, and independence on October 9, 1962. Following independence, the country experienced a turbulent period, including the authoritarian rule of Idi Amin (1971-1979), before Yoweri Museveni came to power in 1986.
