History

The term "Nile Raft Foundation" is not associated with a specific historical institution, but rather refers to the ancient and modern use of rafts on the Nile River for transportation and activities like white-water rafting. Historically, ancient Egyptians used basic rafts made from logs or reeds for travel and trade, a practice that continued through the 17th to 20th centuries using materials like reeds and gourds. Today, modern companies like Nile River Explorers offer guided white-water rafting tours on the Nile, building on the river's long history as a waterway.
