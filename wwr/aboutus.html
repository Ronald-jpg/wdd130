Our History

Nile rafting is a popular water sport in Jinja, Uganda, offering thrilling experiences on world-class rapids, from moderate to extreme grades. It includes professional guides, safety equipment, and options for both experienced adventurers and families.

The activity provides a mix of adrenaline-pumping rides and calm stretches for swimming and sightseeing, with safety kayakers ensuring your well-being throughout the trip.
